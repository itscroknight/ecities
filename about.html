About us

About Us

When visiting a new city, we don't have knowledge about the touristic places of the city and we tend to only visit the most popular places of the city. Knowledge about a place is very important.

To get that knowledge we introduced "Ecities", a web-based application that will provide you with the required information.

Mission & Vision

Mission & Vision

Our Mission is to promote tourism in local areas of the India and increase the popularity of the un-popular local tourist spots. Through Ecities we increase the knowledge about the un-popular touristic spots and encourage people to travel to this places.

Through tourism this places will see boost in economy and employment will be generated. This will promote this areas to develop more and maintain wellness in this areas.

Tourism

The traditionally-described domains of tourism impacts are economic, socio-cultural, and environmental dimensions. The economic effects of tourism include improved tax revenue and personal income, increased standards of living, and more employment opportunities.

Because tourism experiences also involve human interaction, certain impacts may occur. Generally, social impacts in tourism are related to guest-to-host or host-to-guest influences and changes. Studies of these encounters often relate to the Social Exchange Theory, which describe how tourists and hosts’ behaviours change as a result of the perceived benefits and threats they create during interaction.
